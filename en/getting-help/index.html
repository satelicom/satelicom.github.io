FAQs


What is a location system?

A locator shows the position of a vehicle, but doesn’t allow any interaction. It’s in any case a prevention system against theft, because the parking function (if active) allows alarm receiving if the vehicle is moved from the parking location, - used for a consequent check of the position. A locator doesn’t allow to verify if someone enters inside the vehicle trying to steal it and does not allow to activate for example the engine block, direction indicators, hooter and other peculiarities that are typical for a system that is certified as an anti theft device.

Which are the main characteristics of a system to be installed on a car?

Considering safety expectations, installations of more or less complex systems that also correspons to different levels of security can be done - as specified from Anssat (www.anssat.it security levels).

How the system works?

Each system has its own way of working, depending on the specific configuration. The device, according to the configuration, determines the occurrence of the conditions defined as the moment on the event generator and can consequently generate alarms, communications or data storage inside its memory. To each configuration corresponds a specific application. Let’s say, in a simplistic way, that the configuration represents the set of the system evolution and the degree of the technical ability of a company to manage and define a product / service that’s more or less evolved.

Are all the systems identical?

No. The systems differ in various features and for the applications that they’re intended. For heavy vehicles, armored vans, pit vehicles, the characteristic are different than the solutions for the cars, motorbikes, boats or persons. For the applications we mentioned before shall be made a careful analysis of the risks and vulnerabilities in order to provide a product and a service that are optimal for the customer’s needs. In this case Satelicom is available to offer a specific quote and to show the avant-garde solutions developed in these years.

What’s the parking function?

The parking function (called also geo fencing) is a function that, once activated, defines a point and a circular area delimited by a radius (for ex. 300m) inside which the vehicle or the person are allowed to move without generating alarms. If the vehicle goes out of this area alarm is automatically generated an sent on numbers registered inside the device. In this case the user with the LocMe.Mobi function will have the possibility to check directly on his mobile phone the position and the speed of the vehicle to evaluate the risk and in alert the police if necessary. In case the user benefits also the service of the control centre, he’ll be able to ask for a help for a positive solution of the event.

The parking function can be used also on the personal system LOC-ME?

Of course. Pressing on the dedicated button on the LOC ME this functionality is activated for the control of a vehicle (car, truck, camper) but also for the control of children, old people and animals provided with LOC ME.

Does the personal system LOC ME have also other functionalities?

Yes, the personal system LOC ME, apart from the parking function, has also the possibility to send an emergency alarm pressing the SOS button. In this case, in addition to the position, it’s possible to speak in hands free mode with the person that pressed the button, of course if the used SIM card is enabled for VOICE communication.

How can a so small system have so high performances?

The LOC ME system represents in many ways the state of art in the field of the satellite radiolocation. It’s a system where the firmware and the functioning software are embedded inside the phone module and this itself represents the technical skills put on the field to develop an avant-garde product in terms of performance and power consumption to which a great attention was given. Apart from this the group of designers and analysts of the product / service have unique competences, working in this field from several years. Many of the developed solutions that are today on the market and are adopted from the operators in this field are the brainchild of this group of persons that are working in synergy from more than 15 years in this sector.

Does this system needs particular SIM cards?

This system doesn’t need particular SIM cards, but only that the SIM is configured for GPRS communication. There are some SIM (M2M) that don’t allow the voice communication and therefore they’re working only with SMS and GPRS. The advantage of these SIM cards is that the government tax and the provider fee are not due, but normally a small cost for the activation is required, and of course the cost of the data. Therefore when a SIM is chosen it’s very important to evaluate the economical offers of the mobile operators and the compatibility of the system that is not supporting UMTS data. Some phone operators are offering favorable economic conditions defining a data bundle for a fixed month fee.

The system has a history?

Yes, the system stores the points that are saved inside a flash memory. To each stored point, as latitude and longitude, there are associated different parameters, like the speed, the direction and the altitude. The storage of these points has been configured in function of the speed and therefore the lower is the speed the more dense are the points in the history. From the history it’s possible to view the km counter and consequently to check how many km were done for a specific activity.

How precise are these data?

These data are very precise except for the altitude, due to the reasons linked to the conformation of the Earth.The error is between 2.6 and 4.8m, except for some moments when due to different reasons (for example war situations), the GPS signal may disappear.

In case of a theft may I have your help?

If the user benefits of the “help me” service, then he has the possibility to have a control centre working 24 hours per day at his disposal for a support together with the police. In this case the procedures and the software developed from SAETLICOM allows, apart for the user, also the police to locate the position of the vehicle or the person with LOC-ME, in order to proceed in the best way.

Regarding the Privacy how one should behave?

The presence of a location system has always to be communicated to the user of the good/system where the system is installed, in order to comply with the Privacy regulations.

How can I see the saved data of the personal system and change some parameters?

In caso di utilizzo con servizio di centrale, l’utente si dovrà collegare al sito e potrà visualizzare i dati e i relativi report. Un’ulteriore opzione è fornita dal software SATELICOM LOC-ME CENTRALE che potrà essere installato sul PC di casa e quindi, collegando il terminale al PC con l’apposito cavo avente presa USB e mini USB, procederà allo scarico dello storico. Ciò eseguito si potrà visualizzare sul proprio PC i dati sui vari grafici ivi compresa la rappresentazione grafica dello storico su google maps. Il tutto gratuitamente a condizione che l’utente si registri ed ottenga le relative autorizzazioni dal sito google maps nel rispetto delle condizioni di utilizzo ivi descritte. Sempre tramite l’applicativo si potranno modificare dei semplici parametri di funzionamento del sistema.

Yes, some functionalities are changeable through SMS.
