Control System
  • Worldwide localisation: The localization has the worldwide coverage of the used cellular phone service providers (for example: Tim, Vodafone, Wind) and the subscribed roaming agreements.
  • The only Italian operator offering an international service: Satelicom is the only Italian operator offering an international service for the security management, because it is a member (exclusively for Italy) of two international circuits of control centres, Eurowatch and Securi-Track, that cover 98% of Europe.
  • Security network: For a hauler that works with SATELICOM, being the victim of a theft or a robbery in Lisbon, or Vladivostok, or Paris, or Naples or Dortmund - it’s absolutely the same: the management of the event is done in the same mode and with the same timings. Thanks to the partners of the international networks, that Satelicom is a member of, the local police will be promptly warned. With a real time monitoring procedure of the event, also the customer has the chance to follow the development of the situation step by step.
  • Maximum customization: To satisfy the different needs of the customers, Satelicom offers the possibility to create custom solutions of maximum security that are fit to specific needs. There were developed more than 100 different models of customization, with ad hoc schematics and configurations. Therefor the installation technical time may change (from one hour to one day), in relationship to the grade of security and to the desired application.
  • Reliability: Satelicom has a unique experience in the vehicle security: over 350 armored vans each day transports high values in whole Italy, followed from the same security control centre that assists our customers. To ensure the maximum security and continuity of the service, there is a first level informatics infrastructure. It’s based on more than 30 redundant virtual servers, backup batteries and electrical generators, in order to ensure the functioning also during long periods of black out of the public infrastructure.
  • Flexibility: It’s possible to interface the data coming from the radiolocation systems with the company management software through web services, for and optimization of management costs and further needs.

Fleet Management

Web location service with the possibility to interact with a control centre service working 24/7, every day of the year.

With Satelicom Web the customer can control its own fleet, through web, in order to organize in the best way its activity. It is possible to view a single vehicle, a part or the whole fleet with a simple click.

Easy and intuitive reports show the data and offers precise economical analysis of the activity. There are also available interfaces for data communication, developed on a web service technology, for a fast and secure consultation of the information. With the interfacing through web service it is possible to integrate the localization data inside the management and logistic software already available inside the company.

Beside SatelicomWeb there is Satelicom.mobi, ensuring user to know in each moment where your fleet is located, directly on your smartphone or tab. There are available applications for iPhone and Android.



Web Application

The service on web offers the visualization of data on Windows and Mac OS X.

On the web different parameters are presented:

  • Location of a single vehicle;
  • Location of few defined vehicles;
  • Location of all vehicles;
  • Statuses of the vehicle (door status, hatch, trailer hooking, etc.);
  • Visualization of the nearest vehicle, with the distance and the time needed to the arrival, with different parameters;
  • Movements and stops for the cost analysis and the journeys with the possibility to export the data in Excell format;
  • Timings of drivings and stops;
  • Speed chart;
  • Chart of the temperature, for installations with a temperature sensor inside the cargo compartment.
